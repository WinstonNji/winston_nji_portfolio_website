In addition to my studies, I am also the co-founder of WiyoRent, a company dedicated to helping students find affordable housing in Rwanda. Through my academic journey and entrepreneurial efforts, I aspire to contribute positively to the tech landscape in Africa.

FrontEnd Developer

Using HTML and CSS, I designed an engaging interface to communicate the project's goals. While not mobile-responsive, the site featured clear visuals and calls-to-action. This experience enhanced my technical skills and showed how technology can address agricultural challenges.

WiyoRent Co-Founder

As the co-founder and web developer of WiyoRent, I built a mobile-responsive platform showcasing student-focused house listings in Rwanda. The site handles diverse data, including images, listing details, and prices, ensuring comprehensive user access. Since launching, the platform has served over 40 students, and this project has significantly enhanced my technical skills in web development, data management, and responsive design.
